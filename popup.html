Homegate Listings Collector Addon

Homegate Listings Collector:

  • Right-click on a Homegate.ch property listing within the Homegate website.
  • Select the "Homegate Listings Collector" addon from the context menu and choose one of the following options:
    • "Collect Listings" to save all the property listings you are interested in.
      • "Copy to Clipboard" to copy the listings information to your clipboard.
      • "Download as file" to download the listings information as a file on your device.

    Troubleshooting:

  • If you're having trouble collecting the listings, try these steps:
    • Refresh the page.
    • Sort the listings by newest first.
    • Scroll down the page until the last listing.
